Taking Super Smash Bros to a Fantasy eSports Level

Featured Player: Boyblue

November 2017

Real Name: Steve
Main(s): Captain Falcon, Mario
Favorite Character: Captain Falcon
Favorite Food: Cane's Chicken
Contact: boyblue_falcon@gmail.com


Playing Smash since childhood, Boyblue entered the amateur competitive scene in Las Vegas Nevada in 2013. He remembers playing tournaments at Gameworks near the strip as well as at a local arcade where he made many lasting friends. His mains are Captain Falcon and Mario, but he knows how to play pretty much every other character better than most people, much to the chagrin of his friends.

What's Boyblue's advice for improving your play? We interviewed him yesterday and this is what he had to say: Play every day you can. The more you play and the more you study the greats on the many available online services like YouTube and Twitch, the more you'll learn and the more you'll train your hands. Practice as often as you can and listen to your mentors and you'll be sure to improve! He's spent literally thousands of hours playing Super Smash Bros Brawl and Wii U in college and after in the Vegas and Seattle competitive scenes. He even started playing at work at an extracurricular Fight Club he created at his company. We actually sat down at one point near the end of our freshman year of college and calculated how many hours we'd spent on Smash. Let's just say if you translated the results to a minimum wage job we could all have afforded to feed a small country of hungry children or buy some pretty fancy cars. And of course we had to ask the age-old question: Who's your favorite character? Captain Falcon of course! He might not be the highest tier in tournament play but god damn is he a man's man. Falcon all the way! We'd like to thank Boyblue for coming into the studio for our interview today and we wish him luck at the Northwest Majors next weekend.
